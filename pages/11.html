11/Pay it Forward:

The pay it forward coffee is a simple form of care that is often contagious. The act of buying a coffee for the person behind you does not just spread happiness to the person recieving the coffee but also the worker excited to tell them their coffee is paid for. And if you're lucky, it will create a domino effect of kindess that is passed on from person to person.
